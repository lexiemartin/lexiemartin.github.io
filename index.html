What I Do

User Experience

I believe that research is a fundamental component of the creative process. My work in user experience has involved qualitative analysis, wireframing, prototyping, pageflows and documentation.

Design

I create graphics and interfaces in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and Sketch. I've made logos, icons, interfaces, instructional material and advertisements.

Front-end coding

I write code in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I believe in producing lightweight, reusable and legible code. I'm a big fan of jQuery, Zurb Foundation and Twitter Bootstrap.

Who I Am



I've had a lifelong interest in technology, art and the way people think.

I’ve been creating for the web since I was seven years old. My early work featured custom cursors, sparkles and leopard-print backgrounds. I like to think that I’ve matured as a designer since then.

I studied cognitive psychology in college and went on to do post-graduate work in computer science. My professional life so far has been with small companies where I took on a variety of challenges. I've tackled projects in web design, mobile design, front-end development, copyediting, copywriting, advertising and research.

At work, I'm passionate about clear communication, elegant design and human-centered computing. Outside of work I'm an avid sketcher, an occasional writer and a slightly overcompetitive pub trivia player.
